ABOUT US

For over a decade Jeved Group has been providing the ultimate service in luxury real estate and development in one of the most prestigious areas of Limassol.

We aim to cater to each of our clients needs and desires. We want to make sure your journey to your dream home will be trouble free – whether you want to be involved in the construction process or simply add final touches to the design elements of your new home.

If you are looking at purchasing a villa as an investment opportunity our team of legal specialists will also help to guide you through all the processes through start to end in order to guarantee the return on your investment.

We will assist you during the entire process – as we guarantee quality over quantity each step of the way to purchasing your dream villa in Cyprus.
